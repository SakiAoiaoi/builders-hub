# Your First Avalanche Smart Contract

In this challenge, you’ll deploy a **super simple contract** to the Fuji Testnet.  
If you succeed, you’ll earn an NFT certificate!

---

## Get Test Funds

To deploy a contract, you’ll need some test AVAX on the Fuji Testnet.  
Claim free tokens here:  
 [Get Test Funds (Faucet)](https://build.avax.network/docs/dapps/smart-contract-dev/get-test-funds)

** Step-by-step guide:**  
**[Deploy with Remix IDE on Avalanche](https://build.avax.network/docs/dapps/smart-contract-dev/deploy-with-remix-ide)**

## The Task

1. Open [Remix IDE](https://remix.ethereum.org)
